Welcome to IAED 23/24 Project: Parking Lots Management System. Here are the available commands.

q ................................................................. Terminates the program
p [ <park-name> <capacity> <tariff-x> <tariff-y> <tariff-z> ] ..... Creates a parking lot with a billing system or lists existing parking lots
e <park-name> <licence-plate> <date> <hour> ....................... Registers the entry of a vehicle
s <park-name> <licence-plate> <date> <hour> ....................... Registers the exit of a vehicle
v <licence-plate> ................................................. Lists the entries and exits of a vehicle
f <park-name> [ <date> ] .......................................... Shows the billing of a parking lot
r <park-name> ..................................................... Removes a parking lot from the system and all vehicle entries and exits from that parking lot
